HOME GUIDE

🚗 Rear Vehicle Approach

Check behind you immediately for approaching cars.

🚨 Emergency Siren

Move to the side and secure a clear emergency path.

🔥 Fire Alarm

Identify exits and evacuate calmly.

🛴 Fast Moving Objects

Watch for bicycles or kickboards approaching suddenly.

🏠 Indoor Alerts

Check home devices or emergency signals.
